Hi, my name is Sally. I'm a digital media student


and I like to research San Antonio history as a hobby.


My journey began in 2018 after my mom passed.


I began researching her geneolgy and discovered her


family came to San Antonio during the mid 1800s with the


large waves of German and Polish immigrants. This led to


a fascination with San Antonio history in general. I'll gradually


add more photos and possibly blogs to this website as I have


time. I'm open to questions and feedback.
